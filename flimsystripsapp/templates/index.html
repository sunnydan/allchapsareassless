Why is it called that?

Cyanide and Happiness, and the people I like to hang out with have something in common: a silly, good-naturedly irreverant sense of humor. I wanted to find a name for this website that, although entirely unrelated to "Cyanide and Happiness," evoked that same sense of silliness and humor. The name came off a long list of silly and irreverant things that my friends and I have said over the years while hanging out with each other. The list has been, and is still being, recorded by my friend Ben. A couple other hot contenders from that list were "Ostriches and LSD", "Asiago Dreams", and "We are really proud of this meat." We're a kinda weird bunch.
